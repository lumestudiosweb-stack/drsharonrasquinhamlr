Our Expertise

Specialised Services

Comprehensive care using evidence-based protocols and modern technology.

🛡️

Antenatal Care

Complete pregnancy care from conception to delivery — regular monitoring, ultrasounds, nutrition guidance, and risk assessment to ensure a safe and healthy pregnancy journey for mother and baby.

💆

Normal Delivery

Supportive, natural birth care with experienced hands guiding every stage of labour. Dr. Sharon prioritises safe, dignified birthing experiences with personalised pain management and emotional support.

🧪

C-Section

Expert caesarean delivery when medically indicated — performed with precision surgical technique, comprehensive pre-operative planning, and attentive post-operative recovery care.

🧬

Gynaec Check-up

Routine pelvic examinations, Pap smears, STI screening, and preventive wellness evaluations. Early detection and proactive monitoring for lifelong reproductive health.

🔍

PCOS Treatment

Comprehensive, evidence-based management of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome — addressing hormonal imbalances, irregular cycles, fertility concerns, and long-term metabolic health with a tailored treatment plan.

🌞

Menopause Care

Compassionate support through perimenopause and menopause — including Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT), symptom management for hot flashes, mood changes, and bone health, and long-term wellness planning.

🎗️

Cancer Screening & HPV Vaccines

Preventive cervical and gynaecological cancer screening with Pap smears and HPV testing. HPV vaccination for eligible patients — a vital step in protecting against cervical cancer before exposure.

🔬

Gynaec Surgeries

Minimally invasive and open gynaecological surgeries performed with precision:
Fibroid Laparoscopic Surgery — keyhole removal of uterine fibroids with faster recovery.
Hysterectomy — surgical removal of the uterus for fibroids, cancer, or chronic conditions.
Cystectomy — removal of ovarian cysts with preservation of fertility where possible.

🌱

Infertility

Specialised fertility evaluation and treatment for couples facing conception challenges. Investigations include hormonal profiling, ultrasound assessment, and semen analysis — followed by a personalised, evidence-based fertility treatment plan.

👩‍⚕️

Adolescent Health Care

Sensitive, age-appropriate gynaecological care for young women — addressing menstrual irregularities, PCOS, nutritional guidance, and reproductive health education in a safe, non-judgemental environment.
